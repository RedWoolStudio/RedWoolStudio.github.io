Tic Tac Toe Plus Plus

Tic-tac-toe, noughts and crosses, or Xs and Os is a paper-and-pencil game for two players, X and O, who take turns marking the spaces in a 3×3 grid. The player who succeeds in placing three of their marks in a diagonal, horizontal, or vertical row is the winner. But in this game players soon discover that the best play from both parties leads to a draw. So we at RedWoolStudio designed Tic Tac Toe Plus Plus. This game gives you a blend of the original tic-tac-toe and adds upon it. This advanced version of the game requires a bit of practice to win against an opponent. It has various features to keep you engaged. We obviously have Singleplayer and Multiplayer (local and online) modes. We have given the option of playing on a 2D board of width 3 to 10 and also 3D board of width 3 to 6. Other than that it also includes Ultimate/Super tic tac toe and Misère tic tac toe of variable width. We hope you enjoy the game.
